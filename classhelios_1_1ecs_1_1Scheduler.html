Detailed Description

Orchestrates multiple named schedules, handles transitions, and executes them in topological order.

Schedules are identified by types (empty structs satisfying ScheduleTrait). Stages group schedules via InStage and are ordered with OrderStage. When Run() is called, all schedules are executed in topological order; system-local data (commands, messages) is flushed at each schedule boundary.
